Something of Value

Kate Wolin and Anne Charnock will be at Babson College on October 4 & 5 to lead the Something of Value Program.

"Something of Value" is a values-based approach to risk-management education for collegians. The day-long program is led by a traveling volunteer team. Program format includes group discussions of values, a mock trial and teaching confrontation skills. Participants identify risky behaviors specific to their campus environment. Action plans are developed and collegians are empowered to follow-through.
